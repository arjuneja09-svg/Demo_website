Explore South Florida

Discover the vibrant culture and beauty surrounding Azure Palm Resort

Miami Beach

0.1 miles | 2-minute walk

Step directly onto the golden sands of world-famous Miami Beach from our resort. With pristine waters, perfect year-round weather, and vibrant beach culture, Miami Beach offers endless opportunities for sunbathing, swimming, water sports, and beachside dining. The azure waters and soft white sand create the perfect backdrop for your tropical escape.

Swimming Beach Activities Beach Bars Volleyball Courts

Wynwood Walls

6.2 miles | 15-minute drive

Immerse yourself in one of the world's largest open-air street art installations. Wynwood Walls showcases stunning murals and graffiti art from internationally renowned artists. This vibrant neighborhood has transformed into Miami's premier arts district, featuring galleries, boutiques, craft breweries, and trendy eateries. Perfect for art lovers and Instagram enthusiasts alike.

Photo Opportunities Art Galleries Craft Breweries Boutique Shopping

Ocean Drive

0.3 miles | 6-minute walk

Experience the heart of Miami Beach's vibrant culture along the iconic Ocean Drive. This famous boulevard runs parallel to the beach and is lined with Art Deco architecture, outdoor cafes, upscale restaurants, and luxury hotels. By day, enjoy ocean breezes while dining al fresco; by night, experience the electrifying nightlife with live music, dancing, and entertainment.

Dining Nightlife Live Entertainment People Watching

Art Deco Historic District

0.4 miles | 8-minute walk

Step back in time with a stroll through the largest collection of Art Deco architecture in the world. The Miami Beach Architectural District features over 800 preserved buildings from the 1930s and 1940s, showcasing the iconic pastel colors, geometric patterns, and streamline moderne style. Join a guided walking tour to learn about the rich history and architectural significance of this National Register Historic District.

Historic Buildings Walking Tours Photography Architecture History

Lincoln Road Mall

1.2 miles | 5-minute drive

Shop, dine, and people-watch at this renowned pedestrian promenade. Lincoln Road features high-end retailers, boutique shops, art galleries, and an impressive selection of restaurants and cafes. The tree-lined outdoor mall hosts regular art shows, farmers' markets, and live performances, making it a cultural hub for both locals and visitors.

Shopping Cafes Art Galleries Events

Bayside Marketplace & Port of Miami

5.8 miles | 12-minute drive

Explore waterfront shopping and dining at Bayside Marketplace, a festive outdoor mall with live music and bay views. Watch cruise ships depart from the nearby Port of Miami, or book a sightseeing boat tour to see Miami's skyline and celebrity homes along Biscayne Bay.

Waterfront Boat Tours Live Music Shopping
